Software Engineer II | Deliveroo

May 2024 - Present

Deliveroo is a British multinational online food delivery company headquartered in London, United Kingdom, soon to be acquired by doordash. At Deliveroo, I work as a Software Engineer on the Security Engineering team, where I get to build some of the most critical systems that quietly keep everything running safely. Right now, I’m building our Identity Service, which handles all the authentication and authorization across the company. It’s one of those things you don’t notice when it works, but everything breaks when it doesn’t. I’m also designing a Privileged Access Management service to manage ABAC and RBAC roles for all our internal tools. The goal is to make access smart, secure, and effortless for everyone who needs it. It’s challenging work, but knowing that what I build protects both people and products makes it incredibly rewarding. I am using Java, Scala, AWS, terraform and Redis to build these systems.

Senior Software Engineer | Yubi

February 2023 - May 2024

At Yubi, one of the fastest growing fintech startups in India, I worked on building a Credit Application that aimed to solve a major problem in the credit industry. The traditional credit process used to take up to seven days, and what we built brought that down to just a few hours. It wasn’t just about speed — it was about changing how credit moved through the system. I truly believed that my contribution would have a lasting impact on how credit companies operate across India. I worked with a solid tech stack that included Java, Spring Boot, AWS, MongoDB, Kafka, and Redis. It was a challenging experience, but one that gave me a real sense of purpose and growth.
